Angela UGC Posting

Angela UGC Posting is a private workflow used by Angela to publish her own short-form video and social content to connected accounts she authorizes.

About the app

This app helps Angela prepare and publish content to her own social media channels. It is not a public consumer product and is not used to manage other creators' accounts.
